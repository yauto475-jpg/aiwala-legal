Privacy Policy — AIWala Publisher

Last updated: July 2026

1. What This App Does

AIWala Publisher is a personal automation tool that publishes video content I create to my own YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok accounts.

2. Data We Collect

We collect and store access tokens provided by TikTok, Meta, and Google solely to publish content to the connected accounts. No personal data from any third party is collected.

3. How We Use Data

Access tokens are used exclusively to upload and publish video content that I have created, to accounts that I own and control.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

5. Data Deletion

To revoke access and delete stored tokens, disconnect this app from your TikTok/Meta/Google account settings. Tokens are deleted within 24 hours of disconnection.
